About this service
DYAR's Psychosocial Recovery Coach service supports people with psychosocial disability to take charge of their recovery, work toward goals, build daily-living skills, navigate the mental health system, and reduce hospital admissions. We use trauma-informed and recovery-oriented practice, co-create a Wellness Recovery Action Plan (WRAP), and link with treating clinicians.
NDIS funding categories
• Capacity Building — Psychosocial Recovery Coach (line 07_005_0132_8_3)

Where DYAR stores referral information
Referrals received via this form are stored in DYAR's case management system (Splose), DYAR's compliance and document control system (Centro Assist), and Microsoft 365 (email + OneDrive). All systems are hosted in Australia. Access is restricted by role and protected by Multi-Factor Authentication. Full details are in DYAR's Privacy Policy and in the platform-disclosure annexure to the Service Agreement signed at intake.
What happens next
1. DYAR acknowledges receipt within 2 business days.
2. The Service Manager (Mehdi Dabire) reviews the referral and the participant's NDIS plan to confirm fit and funding adequacy.
3. We contact the participant (or nominee) within 5 business days for an intake conversation.
4. If we are the right provider, we send a Service Agreement + Consent Form for signature.
5. Service commences once both forms are signed and any pre-service risk assessment is completed.
If we are not the right fit, we will tell you and (where possible) suggest an alternative provider.
