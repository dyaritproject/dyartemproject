What these are for
==================
- The LEAG (Member + Chair) ads are LIVE — DYAR can publish them straight
  away to recruit real Members of the Lived Experience Advisory Group.

- The 9 Workforce ads are FUTURE ROLES — they show the HDAA auditor where
  and how DYAR will recruit when it hires. They sit on a /careers page
  marked "Future roles — register your interest" until DYAR actually
  starts hiring.

How to put them on the website
==============================
OPTION 1 — Direct paste into a careers page
   1. Open the RTF in TextEdit (Mac) or Word (Win).
   2. Select all (Cmd+A / Ctrl+A) and copy.
   3. Open your WordPress / Squarespace / Wix careers page.
   4. Paste — formatting (headings, bold, lists) carries over.

OPTION 2 — Downloadable PDFs
   1. Open each RTF and save as PDF (File > Export > PDF in TextEdit / Word).
   2. Upload PDFs to your media library.
   3. Add a download button to each role on /careers.

OPTION 3 — Job board cross-posting
   - LEAG Member ad: post to Disability Advocacy WA, Developmental
     Disability WA, peer-support networks, local disability service
     newsletters. Hint: most accept a single Word / PDF + a 100-word
     summary for their newsletter.
   - Workforce ads: post to Seek, Indeed, NDIS Workforce, and LinkedIn
     when DYAR is actively hiring (not now — they're future-roles).

What goes on the /careers page right now
========================================
At a minimum, DYAR's /careers page should contain:
- Plain-language statement: "DYAR is a brand-new NDIS provider. We're not
  hiring paid workers yet, but we ARE recruiting our Lived Experience
  Advisory Group. We'll post job ads here as we grow."
- LEAG Member ad (LIVE — apply now button)
- LEAG Chair ad (LIVE — explain "members first, then Chair")
- Section "Future roles" with the 9 Workforce ads under headers, each
  with a "Register your interest" form (Gravity Forms / Squarespace
  Forms / etc.) — sends to hello@dyar.com.au.
- DYAR equal opportunity + accessibility statement.

Why this matters for the audit
==============================
HDAA will look at DYAR's /careers page (and is welcome to). What they
want to see:
- Evidence DYAR knows what roles it needs to recruit.
- Evidence DYAR has clear, accessible recruitment language.
- Evidence DYAR is committed to lived-experience governance — the LEAG
  ads on the front page are concrete evidence.
- Evidence DYAR meets equal opportunity standards.
